Prasad (Security Guard)

Prasad’s Legacy – a lifetime with Aarti

← Back to all stories
“I have worked at Aarti Home for over twenty years. You see a lot in twenty years. I have known some of the girls from when they were born and when I see them go out to conquer the world putting their pasts behind, to create a new life for themselves, it fills my heart with pride.

I see more happiness and joy every minute at Aarti Home than I ever did at my previous job in a chocolate factory and if that doesn’t say something about Aarti Home’s fantastic contribution to society, then I don’t know what does.”

Prasad has been invaluable to Aarti Home. From watching over the girls to making sure they get home on time, he has always poured his heart and soul into their wellbeing. He stopped many times during the brief interview because his voice would begin to crack with emotion. He is as much a part of the Aarti family as any of us.
