Our Founding Story

How it all began in 1992

"To be orphaned is unfortunate, but to be abandoned is heartbreaking."
— Sandhya, an Aarti child

It was a hot summer day in Kadapa in 1992 when our founder Sandhya Puchalapalli found a two-year-old girl abandoned on the street. Moved by that encounter, she and a small group established what would become Aarti For Girls — a home and a movement that has transformed thousands of lives across three decades.
