Hemalatha

Hemalatha’s Way – from orphan to mentor

← Back to all stories

Orphaned as an infant, R. Hemalatha found a loving family and a new beginning at Aarti Home. Raised by a nurturing community and inspired by those who came before her, Hemalatha never felt the absence of her biological family.

Her passion for learning blossomed at Aarti School, leading her to pursue her dream of becoming a doctor by studying Biology.
