Gayathri

A spark that now lights other classrooms

← Back to all stories

Gayathri was one of those children who absorbed knowledge effortlessly. She arrived at Aarti having missed several years of school but caught up with her peers within months. Her teachers encouraged her to sit competitive exams and she passed with distinction.

Choosing education as her path, Gayathri completed a Bachelor of Education and returned to her home district to work as a schoolteacher. She specifically requested a posting where girls faced pressure to drop out.

In her first year teaching, two girls who had nearly left school went on to top their class. She attributes this to what she experienced at Aarti: a teacher's belief in a child can change everything.

“My teacher at Aarti never gave up on me. I promised myself I would never give up on my students.”

Gayathri is not just a teacher — she is proof of what a girl can become when someone refuses to write her off.

Gayathri continues to work in rural Andhra Pradesh and regularly collaborates with Aarti to identify girls in her community who might need support.
