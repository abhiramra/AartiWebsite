Our Milestone Programs

Four programs, one vision

Every child deserves a loving home, education, and a chance to dream. Aarti's programs ensure abandoned girls find hope, safety, and opportunity.

01 — Care

A home where love comes first

Children thrive in the most loving and safe environments but are put at risk under circumstances like poverty, and lack of stable households and resources. Aarti provides a permanent, nurturing home.

Explore Care →

02 — Educate

Breaking the cycle through learning

We realised that education was the key to breaking the vicious cycle of helpless mothers and abandoned girl children. Aarti School gives every child a quality education regardless of background.

Explore Education →

03 — Empower

Economic independence changes everything

Over our interactions with many women, we have realised that economics play a major role in their not being in control of decision-making. Training women in livelihood skills breaks that dependency.

Explore Empower →

04 — Advocate

Changing minds, changing futures

Most issues surrounding gender equality are the result of ignorance of laws and rights. Aarti's outreach programs educate communities and shift the cultural attitudes that put girls at risk.
