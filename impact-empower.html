Our Story

Empowerment through economics

Our story of empowering women is closely intertwined with the stories of these women who have shaped our goals and motivated us with their courage, strength, and resilience.

Nilofer's Story

Nilofer came to the Women's Centre with no formal income and no sense of what she was capable of. Through our skills training program she discovered an aptitude for tailoring. Within a year she was not only running her own small business but training other women in her neighbourhood. Nilofer's transformation from dependent to earner to teacher captures exactly what the Women's Centre exists to do.
