Meet the stars of Aarti Women's Centre

Our story of empowering women is closely intertwined with the stories of these women who have shaped our goals and motivated us with their courage, strength and resilience.

Nilofer's Story
"My story started when my father sold my textbooks to buy notebooks for my brother. I was uneducated and dependent on my husband and I sought to do something with myself. When I heard that Aarti Home was starting a tailoring program, I enrolled myself. I had a big backlash from my community — as I came home with Rs.50 every day, people started asking me if I was selling myself. Sandhyamma came to our basti and spoke with our community elders. I persisted in spite of the resistance while many of the women who enrolled with me dropped out. I soon became a good tailor and joined the collective."

Our Programs

Skill Building

Fashion design, tailoring, beautician training, embroidery, handicrafts, toy-making, body massage, fabric painting, and block printing — equipping women with marketable skills.

Employability Programs

Computer literacy, communication skills, and resume writing — preparing women to enter and thrive in the workforce.

Women Empowerment Program (WEP Centres)

Community-based centres that serve as safe, empowering spaces where women can access training, counselling, and peer support networks.
