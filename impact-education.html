Kadar Amma's Story
Satram's sudden passing during the COVID-19 pandemic left his daughter, Kadar Amma, and her ailing mother in deep distress with no means of support. Already a student at Aarti School, Kadar Amma was brought to Aarti Village under their COVID-affected family program. Embracing her education and actively participating in leadership classes, she has gained newfound confidence and determination.
Our Programs

1. Aarti School

A community school built on equality, kindness, and compassion. Features include the Know Your Child initiative for personalised learning and a Makerspace and Creative Schooling program fostering innovation and hands-on skills.

2. Aarti Cares

Supporting high-achieving girls at risk of dropping out. Started with 3 girls in 2024, this program has since expanded to 21 students, providing mentorship, resources, and encouragement to continue their education.

3. Children for Children

A global collaboration program that leverages peer learning and international partnerships.
