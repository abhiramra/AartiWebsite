Aarti School

Education as the great equaliser

Aarti English Medium School was established in 2007 with a simple belief: that every child, regardless of gender, background, or economic circumstance, deserves a quality education. In a region where girls are still pulled out of school to be married, this belief is radical — and necessary.

Our school serves children from the Aarti home, family-based care program, and the surrounding community. We combine rigorous academics with creative, child-centred approaches that build confidence alongside knowledge.

Kadar Amma's Story — Strength and Breaking a Cycle of Adversity

Kadar Amma grew up in circumstances that made education seem impossible. Her story is one of persistence against all odds — and a reminder of why Aarti School exists. With support from teachers and house mothers, she not only completed her education but became an inspiration for every child who came after her.

Our Programs

At Aarti, education is our priority

🔍

Know Your Child

A holistic assessment program that identifies each child's unique learning style, strengths, and challenges so teachers can tailor their support effectively.

🎨

Creative Schooling

Learning through art, drama, music, and hands-on projects. Creative schooling builds critical thinking, self-expression, and a love of learning that textbooks alone cannot.

🔧

Makerspace

A dedicated space where children learn to build, experiment, and solve problems with their hands — developing STEM skills and entrepreneurial thinking from a young age.

💼

Aarti Cares: Empowering Future Leaders

A post-school support program that helps graduates navigate higher education, career pathways, and the transition to independent adult life.

🌍

Children for Children

A global initiative connecting Aarti children with student volunteers worldwide for knowledge-sharing, IT skills, and collaborative projects that open minds and build global citizenship.

📚

Digital & Physical Libraries

Modern libraries — physical and digital — give children access to a world of knowledge beyond the classroom, fostering a lifelong habit of reading and exploration.
