Aarti is a way of life, one that emphasizes women's place and value in our society.

Aarti is driven by a profound belief in the power and potential of girls and women. We envision a society where outdated mindsets are replaced with celebration, care, and education, allowing women to confidently make their own choices.

Our Vision

A society with a changed mindset regarding girls and women who, being aware of their rights, are celebrated, cared for and educated, and independently make informed choices.

Our Mission
  • Empowering girls to become self-sufficient.
  • Actively campaigning against the abhorrent practices of female foeticide and infanticide.
  • Upholding the intrinsic value of every girl child and woman.
  • Providing women with the power of education and enabling them to confidently express themselves.
Guiding Principles
Integrity
Doing what is right, always.
Honesty
Truth in every action and word.
Commitment
Steadfast dedication to our mission.
Accountability
Owning our actions and outcomes.
We Ensure That
  • Values of Integrity, Compassion and Empathy are firmly instilled.
  • Children's insecurities are addressed with love.
  • Children grow up with security of shelter, food and healthcare.
  • Children are confident, have high self-esteem and bond.
  • Children are taken seriously, their voices heard.
  • Children are encouraged to dream.
  • Children's confidentiality is maintained.
