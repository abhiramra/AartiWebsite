About Aarti

Our Mission

"Aarti is a way of life, one that emphasizes women's place and value in our society."

Aarti is driven by a profound belief in the power and potential of girls and women. We envision a society where outdated mindsets are replaced with celebration, care, and education — allowing women to confidently make their own choices.

Vision

A world that celebrates every girl

A society with a changed mindset regarding girls and women who, being aware of their rights, are celebrated, cared for and educated, and independently make informed choices.

Mission

What drives us every day

  • ✓

    Empowering girls to become self-sufficient.

  • ✓

    Actively campaigning against female foeticide and infanticide.

  • ✓

    Upholding the intrinsic value of every girl child and woman.

  • ✓

    Providing women with the power of education and the confidence to express themselves.
